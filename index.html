Our Executive Stories

HRM OBA ADEWUMI ADENIJI ONIBUDO EKUN OF IBUDO AYETORO

The Entire People Of Ibudo Ayetoro Kingdom On Behalf Of Our Kabiyesi, Hrm Oba Adewumi Adeniji, The Onibudo Ekun Of Ibudo Ayetoro Congratulate Kabiyesi's Alma Mata Anglican Comprehensive High School, Ipaja.
We congratulate the President of the Old Student Association, Revd Femi Lapite and the entire Executive members and students of our dear school.

HRM Oba Kolawole Egundupe, Ilufemiloye I, Olu of Ipaja Kingdom

on behalf of the entire good people of IPAJA rejoice with the Anglican Comprehensive High School Old Students Association and the Management, Students of the School on the occasion of your Golden Jubilee Anniversary. My people are indeed happy and proud that the School is located in our peaceful environment..
